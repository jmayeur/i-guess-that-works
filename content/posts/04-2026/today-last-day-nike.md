Today was my last day at Nike. There is no author who would have written my story as it has come to pass. The list of "and then this"s is long, and at every inflection; whether up or down I have been incredibly lucky to meet those who challenged, those who nurtured and those who have inspired me. 

Today I have been given the opportunity to take my accumulated learnings and find the next puzzle. I will look back at my Nike time with incredible fondness. It was the ideal proving ground for me to take my jumble of skills and create a career out of solving the challenges that found their way to me. 18 years of Just Do It, and now, here's to the next incredible list of "and this"s.

## Gratitude

To everyone I've worked with at Nike, no matter where you land in this current storm, know that I am grateful for your time. I have only made it this far because of those who ran the race with me. I would gladly solve new problems with any of you. I'm here to learn, listen and support you all through our next steps.
